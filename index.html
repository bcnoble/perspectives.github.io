Learning in VR.

To promote empathy in the workplace, AMP offers an immersive VR experience that allows the user to feel what it’s like to walk in someone else’s shoes. In this experience, you’ll meet Marisol and get a firsthand glimpse into the gender discrimination she faces in the tech industry. Across multiple industries, the case for VR is clear. Whether it’s doctors and their patients, designers and clients, or a salesforce and its customers, empathy is the key to more efficient and effective relationships. And HR professionals around the globe are successfully harnessing VR as a powerful tool to achieve it.

Workplace Diversity.

The power of workplace diversity is undeniable. Diverse companies are 1.7x more likely to innovate their market, 70% more likely to capture new markets, and they make better business decisions 87% of the time. What may be even more important is the impact of NOT having a diverse workplace. In a Deloitte survey, 80% of employees report that inclusion is an important factor in choosing an employer. 72% say they would leave their current employer for a more inclusive workplace. And in fact, 30% of younger respondents had already done so.
